4-Day Alleppey Houseboat Trip

Trip Overview

Experience the magical backwaters of Alleppey on this romantic 4-day houseboat journey. Enjoy serene waters, delicious Kerala cuisine, and breathtaking sunsets.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Alleppey, houseboat check-in, backwater cruise.
  • Day 2: Explore local villages, traditional Kerala lunch, fishing activities.
  • Day 3: Visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, candlelight dinner on the boat.
  • Day 4: Sunrise cruise, breakfast on deck, departure.

Package Includes

  • Luxury houseboat stay
  • All meals included
  • Private candlelight dinner
  • Sunset & sunrise backwater cruise
